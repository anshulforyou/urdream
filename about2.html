About Us

Who we are?

Today, everyone is in a race of making money, and why not? After all money is what drives the world. But in this fast pacing world people often forget that the most important thing is helping each other as humans.

So we thought of doing something where we can contribute to the society to some extent and therefore we came up with the idea to launch UrDreamss. We have pledged to contribute to which is a Non-Profit Organization dedicated to the brave Armed Forces of our country.

UrDream

is the one stop solution to all the challenges that an aspiring student who wants to pursue graduation and make a future in foreign countries generally face. Our team consists of extremely capable counselors who leverage their time and experience to make a student’s future secure. We do not charge any consultation fee for the time that our consultants spend on the candidate before finalization of his/her candidature with the respective university.

The complete amount payable to us will be officially and legally transferred to the partner NGO’s account by the depositor himself and he/she will also receive an official government certified 80G certificate.

*NOTE: No money is to be credited into the account of UrDream separately.
